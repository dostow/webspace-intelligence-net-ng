We are Consultants

Intelligence Developement Consulting Group Limited is the pre-eminent global strategy and transaction advisory consultancy that enables organisations desirous of leading their respective industries, realise and maintain competitive advantage over their peers, through the utilisation of cutting-edge innovation and applied intelligence.

Our Mission

We seek to unlock and unleash the latent potentialities embedded within our clients’ organisations, within their systems and people, for their own benefit; and that of global development and harmony. this approach enables us construct bespoke value propositions by combining their own unique perspectives with the strategic insights and diverse expertise of our highly skilled and motivated professional staff.

The end game of our interventions is the delivery of optimal performance. we aim to get it right the first time, every time, on time, with minimal effort and resources.

Our History

Intelligence development consulting group limited was founded by leading strategy consultant, Ehizogie Benjamin L. EROMOSELE. incorporated on 13th november 1998, the company had previously focused on providing sovereign consulting services and strategic support to government[s] and public sector entities. however, a redefinition of our raison d’étre has seen our company increasingly engaging and collaborating in the resolution of more diverse institutional and corporate challenges.

The ownership of intelligence development consulting group limited is privately held, with principled corporate governance standards institutionalised as the foundation of organisational culture at all levels.

Meet Our Team

Our consultants – Our most strategic resource – are imbued with versatile educational qualifications and diverse project experience.

Ehizogie Benjamin L. EROMOSELE, Founder & Principal

Mr. Eromosele obtained a bachelor of science (hons) second class upper degree in business administration from the prestigious university of benin business school. He has extensive banking experience in corporate finance, treasury, foreign exchange dealing services and international operations. he has attended numerous training programmes such as central bank of nigeria [cbn] seminars and international banking courses including the renowned citibank treasury management [bourse] course in february 1991.

Mr. Eromosele resigned voluntarily from the banking sector in 1993 as chief foreign exchange [fx] dealer and head of international treasury at pinacle commercial bank limited.

Since then, he has served as a consultant to many private sector entities and several government departments/agencies. over the past decade, mr. Eromosele has acted as policy adviser, analyst and advocate for several governments on matters of strategic interest to the respective sovereign states. he serves on the board of numerous reputable organisations. Mr. Eromosele is the founder/chief executive of intelligence development consulting group limited.

Ohiomoje A. Ifiabor,
Director

Mr. Ifiabor earned a master of banking and finance [mb&f] from university of lagos and a master of business administration [mba] from university of nigeria, nsukka. in a remarkable, almost thirty [30]-year career within nigeria’s banking and finance industry, Mr. Ifiabor rose to the esteemed position of executive director at equatorial trust bank.

Mr. Ifiabor has attended several domestic and international courses and received countless performance awards from the banking/finance industry and superlative accolades from his peers.

One of the most sought-after corporate turn-around engineers, mr. Ifiabor initiated and anchored the transformation of a nigerian mortgage banking institution to an international brand through restructuring and substantial foreign equity investment, thereby making history as the first mortgage banking institution in nigeria to hold such foreign equity investment. mr. Ifiabor is a director at intelligence development consulting group limited.
