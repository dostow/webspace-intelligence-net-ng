Our Founder & Principal

Ehizogie Benjamin L. EROMOSELE, Founder & Principal

Mr. Eromosele obtained a Bachelor of Science [Hons.] Second Class Upper degree in Business Administration from the prestigious University of Benin Business School. He has extensive banking experience in Corporate Finance, Treasury, Foreign Exchange [FX] Dealing Services and International Operations.

He has attended numerous training programmes such as Central Bank of Nigeria [CBN] Seminars and International Banking courses including the renowned Citibank Treasury Management [Bourse] Course in February 1991.

Mr. Eromosele resigned from the banking sector in 1993 as Chief Foreign Exchange [FX] Dealer and Head of International Treasury at Pinacle Commercial Bank Limited.

Since then, Mr.Eromosele has served as a Consultant to many Private Sector entities and several Government Departments and Agencies.

For several decades, Mr. Eromosele has acted as Policy Adviser, Analyst and Advocate for several Governments on matters of strategic interest to the respective sovereign states.

Mr. Eromosele serves on the board of numerous reputable organisations and is the Founder/Chief Executive of Intelligence development consulting group .
